# Earn-to-Play games

With a total of 7 games in our pipeline, all will belong to the new gamic economy – the ”Play-to-Earn” model.  Players in Sugarverse are rewarded for their time, efforts, and skills in the game. This concept gives players ownership rights over all in-game items and incentivizes them to play and progress through the game.
